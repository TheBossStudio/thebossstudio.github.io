Game Showcases

Bad Dream - Horror Game

A first-person horror experience crafted in Unity, featuring dynamic object interaction, immersive sound design, and real-time lighting. Players must navigate a dark, atmospheric basement — uncover clues, interact with the environment, survive jumpscares, and find the hidden key to escape.

Last Drop - FPS Survival

A FPS survival game where you fight off enemy shooters, hostile bandits, and two types of zombies — weak runners and massive tanks with heavy health. Bandits can be turned into bodyguards to fight by your side. Features a full base-building system with placeable walls, floors, stairs, and doors, letting players craft defenses in a world that's always trying to kill them.

Racing Car Parking - Parking Game

A racing car parking game combining speed and precision. Navigate tight courses with realistic controls and park high-performance cars through increasingly difficult levels designed to test timing, reflexes, and accuracy under pressure.

Dark Dayz - Top Down Survival

A top-down survival game where players face zombie waves while scavenging to survive. Build and upgrade a satellite system to unlock teleportation between safe zones and high-risk areas, adding strategy and freedom to exploration in a deadly world.
